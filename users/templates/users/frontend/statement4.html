Instant Noodle Infographic for WhatIF Foods



Create and design an infographic to highlight the wonderful properties of WhatIF Foods Noodles and why they are special. The design should bring out the technology behind our noodles, their nutritional properties and how they compare to other similar products in the market. The infographic should also demonstrate how this product is more environmentally friendly to the planet.

{% if user.username %} Challenge Background

The poster should include:

  1. Logo
  2. A simple and engaging explanation of our mission (see ppt brief for further detailed information)
  3. Name of our noodles: “Moringa Noodles with Sesame Garlic seasoning” and “BamNut Noodles with Sweet Hot seasoning”
  4. The USPs of the noodles (their special powers: high protein/contains iron). Note the 2 noodles have different USPs (see ppt brief).
  5. Outlining the technology behind WhatIF Foods noodles that make them healthier! (see ppt brief).
  6. Nutritional comparison of our noodles compared to market benchmarks.
  7. Taglines of our noodles: ‘In the mood for noods?’, ‘Not deep-fried like the other guys’, bonus points if you can come up with playful lines!
  8. Our social media channels: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Email: hello@whatif-foods.com
When attempting this challenge, participants may consult with the WhatIF Foods Team through an online meeting. They will need to adhere to the following steps:
  1. Email a request for consultation to hello@whatif-foods.com
    1. Subject for the mail: CREATION 2021 Consultation
    2. Suggest a meeting date, time and duration for the meeting (at least 15 minutes, at most 30 minutes)
    3. CC email to creation2021@nussucommit.com
  2. WhatIF Foods will confirm whether they are available for the online meeting on the suggested date. Meeting links will be provided together with this reply.
  3. Once confirmed, prepare a list of questions for enquiries beforehand for a smooth consultation and responsibly attend the meeting.
{% endif %} Company Background



“Our team became disillusioned with our current food system's status quo in creating and maintaining the paradoxical existence of hunger, obesity, nutritional deficiency, and the destruction of our planet. In 2013, we left our corporate jobs to change the world one meal at a time.

Our belief is simple: food should always be nourishing to us and the planet, whether it is a hearty breakfast, a rushed office lunch, or a midnight snack.

Finding our roots in nature, we harness the power of Future Fit Crops, which are nutritionally dense, climate-resilient, resource-efficient, and can be grown on degraded lands, to innovate food we know and love. After years of research, we are proud to show you our take on a better future of food: one that is not only nourishing, diverse, and resilient but also empowers farming communities.”
